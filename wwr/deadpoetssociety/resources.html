Below you can find videos on why thinking freely is important, and how expressive you can get while you stand for what you believe. Let your mind think of new ways to say mundane things, avoid using the word ‘very’ because it’s lazy. A man is not very tired, he is exhausted. Don’t use very sad, use morose. Savor language and words because no matter what anyone tells you, words and ideas have the power to change the world.
