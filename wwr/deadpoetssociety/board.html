WHO ARE WE AS POETS?

Discover our club's meeting times and venues, ensuring you can mark your calendars for real-time gatherings where the magic of poetry comes to life in shared spaces and collective inspiration. Stay in the loop by exploring information on our upcoming poetry competitions, challenges, and events. These opportunities not only provide a platform for you to showcase your talent but also offer a chance to receive well-deserved recognition within our vibrant community. The competitions are held regularly, giving you an environment where your creativity can bloom and be celebrated. To become an active participant, simply submit your entries or inquiries to the provided email address. We eagerly await your contribution to our growing tapestry of poetic expression. Join us in forming lasting connections, finding inspiration, and celebrating the profound impact of words. Become a part of our poetry-loving family, where every word you share holds the power to create, inspire, and connect. We look forward to welcoming you into our community, where the beauty of poetry awaits your unique voice.
